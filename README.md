# Physics Labs

This repository contains a selection of laboratory reports from my undergraduate physics studies. These projects focus on the application of statistical methods, numerical fitting, and computational analysis to classical physical systems.




### 01. Electric Circuits Analysis
*   **Topics:** Series/Parallel networks, Voltage Dividers, Wheatstone Bridges, and RC filters.
*   **Key Techniques:** Error propagation, frequency response modeling, and circuit linearization.

### 02. Mechanical Oscillations
*   **Topics:** Resonance and phase shift in damped mechanical systems (Pohl's wheel).
*   **Key Techniques:** Non-linear least squares fitting (Levenberg-Marquardt), and resonance curve characterization.

### 03. Rotational Dynamics & Gyroscopes
*   **Topics:** Precession, Nutation, and Moments of Inertia in three-axis gyroscopes.
*   **Key Techniques:** Conservation of energy vs. kinematic modeling, and friction analysis.

### 04. Thermodynamics & Heat Capacity Ratio
*   **Topics:** Determination of the Adiabatic Index ($\gamma$) and the speed of sound.
*   **Key Techniques:** Ultrasonic field characterization and Rüchardt's method.

### 05. Electromagnetism: Magnetic Fields
*   **Topics:** Field distribution in Solenoids and Helmholtz coil pairs.
*   **Key Techniques:** Biot-Savart law verification and numerical mapping of magnetic flux density.

### 06. Real Gas Thermodynamics
*   **Topics:** Isotherms of Sulfur Hexafluoride ($SF_6$) and critical point phenomena.
*   **Key Techniques:** Van der Waals modeling and Maxwell construction analysis.

### 07. Coupled Mechanical Oscillators
*   **Topics:** Normal modes and beat dynamics in coupled pendulums.
*   **Key Techniques:** Automated peak detection and coupling strength determination.

## Skills
*   **Languages:** Python (NumPy, SciPy, Pandas, Matplotlib)
*   **Data Analysis:** Non-linear regression, Error propagation, and Signal processing (Peak detection).
*   **Experimental Tools:** Digital Oscilloscopes, Rotary Sensors, and Ultrasonic Transducers.
